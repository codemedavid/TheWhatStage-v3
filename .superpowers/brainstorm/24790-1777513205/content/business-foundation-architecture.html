Why this is the base

  • Each product becomes its own searchable source.
  • Action pages read normalized product data instead of JSON blobs.
  • RLS can protect every row by owner.
  • Orders are captured without payment risk.

Intentionally later

  • Payment methods and bank instructions.
  • Inventory reservation and fulfillment automation.
  • Property, digital product, and services editors.
  • Messenger carousel sending API polish.
